Briefing

The mission opens with the history panel (message_history_iunet) and the briefing popup (message_mission_dendera):

Pharaoh Khufu has taken the throne, and, as foretold by the seers of Horus and Ra, our people have already begun to suffer under his oppression. Khufu has ordered that a Royal Governor be dispatched at once to Iunet, to defend our land against Kushite invaders.

Iunet can support a small fishing industry. If the Kushites invade by water, the river may become unsafe for fishing boats, and shore areas may be better used to support a fleet of warships. If food becomes scarce, cattle may be raised for meat, though the herds require much straw for fodder — straw you will also need to make bricks for your mastaba.

The finest ivory may be obtained via Byblos, trading with Assyria and Ur. And Pharaoh has ordered countless quarries dug at On — it is rumored he plans a colossal building project on the plateau outside Rostja.

Iunet is the ancient Egyptian name for Dendera in Upper Egypt, later famous for its Ptolemaic temple of Hathor. In the campaign it is the military arm of the Khufu-era fork: a frontier town defending against Kushite raids while building a single noble mastaba and thriving on fishing, cattle, gold, and the new luxury trade in ivory.

Strategy

Iunet balances a large population (4 000), a demanding kingdom rating (65), and recurring Kushite raids around a single-mastaba monument goal.

Phase 1 — Food from river and herds

Fishing Wharves feed the city while the river is safe; add Cattle Ranches for meat (and Pharaoh's year-3 request), but note cattle need straw fodder — import straw from Abu or Men-nefer. Grain and barley farms round out the food supply for 4 000 citizens.

Phase 2 — Gold and the mastaba

Establish Gold Mines early — gold and granite are your export earners toward the kingdom rating. A Granite Quarry supplies both exports and the granite the small mastaba needs. Build the mastaba once granite flows; it alone satisfies the monument goal (9).

Phase 3 — Defense

Kushites raid around years 2 and 5. Wall the city, keep a Military Academy and trained companies, and build Warship Wharves — even though enemy ships aren't modelled yet, warships help patrol and the wharves match the briefing's intent. Don't stand the army down between raids.

Phase 4 — Culture, prosperity, luxury

Culture 30 and prosperity 30 need schools, a library, temples, entertainment (including a Senet House), and beautification. Import ivory-as-luxury-goods via Byblos to lift house values toward prosperity 30.

Tips

  • Cattle need straw — never let the straw import lapse or meat production (and the year-3 request) stalls.
  • Gold is the kingdom-rating engine here; each fulfilled Pharaoh request is +8.
  • The mastaba needs granite, not plain stone — quarry or import granite before starting it.
