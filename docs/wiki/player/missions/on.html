Briefing

The mission opens with the history panel (message_history_on) and the briefing popup (message_mission_heliopolis):

Pharaoh Khufu has taken the throne, and our people have already begun to suffer under his oppression. It is rumored he plans a colossal building project on the plateau outside Rostja, to usurp the renown his father Snofru earned from his two noble pyramids.

Khufu has ordered a cluster of quarries dug at Tura, where rich deposits of fine white limestone have been discovered. You must also build three mastaba tombs for the nobles of this region. The quarrying settlement you found shall be named "On", and shall serve as a plentiful source of limestone for many years.

The finest ivory may be obtained via Byblos, trading with Assyria and Ur. With the arrival of such rare luxury goods, On is sure to prosper.

On is the ancient Egyptian name for Heliopolis (Iunu), the great cult centre of the sun god Ra. In the campaign it is the peaceful, high-culture arm of the Khufu fork: a limestone-quarrying town that builds three noble mastabas and reaches the highest culture and prosperity targets so far, without a single invasion.

Strategy

On trades military pressure for economic and cultural demands: the highest culture target so far (40), three mastabas to build, and a large limestone-export economy — all in peace.

Phase 1 — Limestone and food

Limestone Quarries are the backbone: they supply the mastabas, Pharaoh's requests, and a lucrative export. Grain and barley farms feed 4 000 citizens; barley also feeds the Breweries that keep the Senet Houses and housing supplied.

Phase 2 — Three mastabas

Small mastabas need bricks — set up Clay Pits, a Bricks Workshop, and a straw supply (farm by-product or import). Build all three; together they satisfy the monument goal. Work Camps and the three guilds keep construction moving.

Phase 3 — Culture 40 and prosperity 35

This is the highest culture goal to date. Blanket the city with Scribal Schools, Libraries, temples (Ra and Bast especially), and full entertainment including Senet Houses. Prosperity 35 comes from evolved housing — import luxury goods (ivory) via Byblos and keep taxes and services healthy.

Tips

  • No forts are strictly required — invest the labor in culture and the mastabas instead.
  • Limestone is both the export earner and a Pharaoh request; size quarrying above export demand.
  • Bast temples raise culture and keep the cat-goddess content — useful in a peaceful showcase city.
